About Me

Hi, I'm an aspiring Software Engineer based in London, and technology, gaming, web design/development have always been key areas of interest of mine. After my A-Levels, having decided the traditional route of going to university was not for me, I decided to take a gap year to work, while I thought about and explored possible career paths and what I wanted to do in life.

Just before the onset of the pandemic, I decided web development is someting I wanted to do long term and was one of the few industries that was largely unaffected by the lockdown. Thus, I embarked upon a journey to get the basics of web development down, scouring the internet for free resources, such as FreeCodeCamp and CodeAcademy, as well as applying for positions, roles and bootcamps that would allow me to develop these skills.
